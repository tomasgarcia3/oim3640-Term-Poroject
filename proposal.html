Project Proposal

What is your website about?

My website is about Kitesurfing, more specifically will be about traveling the world looking for places to practice the sport. I will like to show people the best locations around the world to kitesurf.

What is the objective of your website?

Essentially my website will be a place for people looking to travel the world while being able to kitesurf. This is something I am passionate about. I don't take a lot of vacations a year but when I do I like to practice my favorite hobby which is kitesurfing. Along the way, I have found some travel blogs but is sometimes difficult to find all the options in one place. The purpose of this website will be to talk about 5 of the 7 continents in the world, and show which are some of the best sports to kitesurf in those regions.

Who is your target audience?

My target audience is kitesurfers like myself who look to spend a vacation in a foreign country and take advantage of practicing the sport there.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

The following list of continents will represent a page on my website. The contents of each page will have the same topic but differ in information. I will talk about the best kitesurfing spots in countries located on these continents. The pages will include images and information about these places. I don't know how detailed the information would be but I will reference what makes these places so special, how easy it is to get to those places, and provide basic pricing information

  2. South America
